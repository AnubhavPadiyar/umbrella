Seismic Zone Guide Bureau of Indian Standards IS 1893
Zone V
Very High Damage Risk. Peak Ground Acceleration >0.36g. Includes Chamoli, Uttarkashi, Rudraprayag. Site of 1991 Uttarkashi (M6.8) and 1999 Chamoli (M6.4) earthquakes.
Zone IV
High Damage Risk. PGA 0.24g. Includes Pithoragarh, Tehri Garhwal. Tehri Dam sits in this zone — highest dam in Asia.
Zone III
Moderate Damage Risk. PGA 0.16g. Includes Haridwar and lower Ganga plains. Lower tectonic activity than upper Himalaya.
