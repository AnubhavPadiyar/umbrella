Uttarakhand Fire Season Forest Survey of India data
Feb — Jun
Peak fire season. Dry conditions, low humidity, strong westerly winds. Chir pine forests in lower Himalayas highly flammable. Most FSI fire alerts recorded in March–May.
Oct — Jan
Moderate risk post-monsoon. Dry deciduous forests shed leaves creating fuel load. Less severe than spring season but significant in years with weak monsoon.
Jul — Sep
Monsoon season — very low fire risk. High rainfall and humidity suppress fires. Green vegetation acts as natural firebreak across Himalayan slopes.
2024 Season
Over 1,000 forest fire incidents recorded in Uttarakhand. Nainital, Pauri Garhwal and Almora worst affected. State declared forest fire emergency in April 2024.
